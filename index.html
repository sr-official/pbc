About Us

Who We Are

PCIU Business Club (PBC) is an approved student organization of Port City International University (PCIU). PBC is a learning organization where a group of selected students of PCIU work together to arrange various business-related events, competitions, trips, social events, and help to create a bond between students of similar interest through sharing of individual knowledge, ideas, and creativity.

Our Mission

To Create a unity of business administration of university. To Inspire the students, do something outside the bookish knowledge. And explore them all the sector

Our Vision

Developing Business administration of PCIU that the students can lead all the sectors and recognize the club among other universities.
